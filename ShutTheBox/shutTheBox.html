Shut the Box

The Rules

  1. Each turn, you roll the dice (or die) and select one or more boxes which sum to roll of your value.
  2. You will not be allowed to pick the boxes which you choose on subsequent turns.
  3. When the sum of boxes which are left is less than or equal to six, you'll only roll a single dice.
  4. When you cannot make a move or you give up, the sum of boxes which are left give you score.
  5. Lower scores are better and a score of 0 is called shutting the box.
